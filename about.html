ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Samantha Bello, a 3D Technical Artist specialized in modeling, sculpting, and texturing for video games, film, and digital projects. My experience as a freelance professional has allowed me to create characters, environments, and props optimized for real-time engines, ensuring high visual quality and attention to detail.

I am proficient in tools such as Maya, ZBrush, Blender, and Substance Painter, managing 3D scenes, creating efficient UVs, and performing production-ready retopology. I also have experience in PBR texturing and exporting materials for Unity and Unreal Engine, guaranteeing clean and optimized integration.

I'm passionate about transforming ideas into digital realities, applying creativity and technique to every project to exceed expectations.
